Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Multiple URL Opener work?

Simply paste your URLs in the text area, one per line, and click "Open All URLs" to open them all at once, or "Open Randomly" to open them in random order.

Is there a limit to how many URLs I can open?

While there's no strict limit, we recommend opening no more than 100-200 URLs at once to prevent browser performance issues.

How does the domain analytics feature work?

We automatically track and display the most frequently opened domains to help you understand your browsing patterns.
